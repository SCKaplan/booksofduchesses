Books of Duchesses


Mapping Women Book Owners, 1350-1550



S.C. Kaplan (UCSB)
&
Sarah Wilma Watson (Independent Scholar)

Books of Duchesses collects, organizes, and presents data related to late-medieval laywomen and their books. Through an interactive map of Europe, users are able to visualize networks of manuscripts, texts, and readers and explore the libraries and peregrinations of woman book owners. For more information about our methodology and how to use the project see About/Usage.
